LNbits has published two bounties for developers to claim for the development of two Nostr Wallet Connect features for LNbits.

There are 1,500,000 sats waiting to be award to anyone who builds these features for LNbits.

LNbits is looking for a developer to build a NWC wallet service extension and a NWC funding source for LNbits.

These two features will allow people to user their own LNbits app running on a home node, VPS, SaaS or anywhere else to interoperate with any other apps that use NWC.

The bounty specifications are linked below

[Build a Nostr Wallet Connect Funding Source for LNbits](naddr1qvzqqqr4gupzqljn39ud4gy4md5cm52vxwj4wcqae6q807g3lzgpm2f4rwjes74kqqxnzdejxqcrqdp58qunsdf3z74727)

[Build a Nostr Wallet Connect Wallet Service Extension for LNbits](naddr1qvzqqqr4gupzqljn39ud4gy4md5cm52vxwj4wcqae6q807g3lzgpm2f4rwjes74kqqxnzde3xsmrgvpkxq6nvvfht0avww)
